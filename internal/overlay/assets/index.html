engelOS overlays

Add as a Browser Source in OBS. Recommended 1920×1080, transparent.

  • /overlay/events Bottom-anchored live activity feed: chat plus sub/raid/streak alerts.
  • /overlay/alerts Centered single-alert player for subs, raids and streak milestones.
  • /overlay/leaderboard Corner panel of top viewer streaks, auto-refreshing every 30 seconds.
  • /overlay/now-playing Bottom-left now-playing card for the channel's song requests.
  • /overlay/song-player YouTube player that auto-plays the bot-managed song-request queue. Needs ?channel=.
  • /overlay/wrapped Shareable Spotify-Wrapped-style recap card. Needs ?channel= (and optional ?viewer=).
Theming: append ?theme=dark|light and ?accent=<hex> to any URL. Defaults: theme=dark, accent=#a970ff.
